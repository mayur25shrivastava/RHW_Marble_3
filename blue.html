BLACK LIMESTONE PAVING 900X600

Black Limestone Paving 900X600 found in the sedimentary rock and made up of Calcite and aragonite. Limestone Slabs and Tiles highly water soluble in sedimentary rock.

Appearance of Limestone

These limestone are naturally exist in earth deposit under normal soil. Under the soil these limestone available. Limestone fits in amazingly with outdoor and garden area.

Personalizing your garden is eay with limestone pavers. while there aren't as many color options as sandstone or slates, these are still many beautiful options and shades of limestone.

one of the key differences is that sandstone is considerably more porous, meaning it absorbs a bit more water than limestone. That why we prefer limestone slabs are better for UK weather.

If you should consider what your paving will look like when wet. In the UK, you might find it rains more days that its dry, and it will your limestone paving looks slightly different from others products.
